# 🌸 Heal Within

A conflict resolution application designed to facilitate empathy and understanding between two groups: those who feel hurt and those who caused the hurt.

## Features

- **Two Group Perspectives**: Separate flows for "Hurt Group" and "Who Hurted Group"
- **Guided Reflection**: Three key questions to promote self-reflection:
  1. What happened?
  2. How did you feel?
  3. What do you want now?
- **Shared Understanding**: Display reflections to encourage mutual understanding and dialogue
- **Clean, Calming UI**: Modern gradient design with emoji-based visual distinctions
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on mobile and desktop devices

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Select your group (Hurt Group or Who Hurted Group)
3. Answer the three reflection questions
4. View your reflection
5. Share with the other group to understand each other's perspective

## How It Works

### Screen Flow
- **Start Screen**: Choose which group you belong to
- **Form Screen**: Answer three guided questions based on your perspective
- **Result Screen**: View your reflection and prompt for dialogue with the other group
- **Restart**: Option to begin the process again
